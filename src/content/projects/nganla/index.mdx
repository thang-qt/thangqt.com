Ngàn Lá is the study hub I wish we had in high school. It collects exams for grades 10–12 across every subject, textbook, and curriculum, so students can go from discovery to practice without jumping between PDFs and social groups.

The focus is simple: find fast, practice smart, review deep. Students can browse by textbook or topic, take exams online, save the papers they care about, and then replay their full session to see exactly how they moved through each question.

## What students can do

- Find exams by grade, subject, curriculum, or textbook
- Practice focused question sets by topic
- Take exams online in single-question or continuous modes
- Review results with timeline insights and session replay
- Save favorite exams and track progress over time
- Get quick help from Cú Mèo, the built-in AI assistant
